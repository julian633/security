SJADU SOLUTIONS GROUP,

SJADU Solutions Group specialises in offering companies with hygiene and cleaning solutions tailor-made to each unique business environment. Founded in 2018, SJADU Solutions Group is equipped to provide customers with industrial, commercial office cleaning and outdoor and events cleaning support. While Hygiene is not the only focus that adds to a sound working atmosphere, SJADU Solutions Group also provides site enhancement add-ons such as plant and planters, where we either offer once off purchases or monthly rental options with maintenance included.

Our Services

Industrial and Office Cleaning

Each office environment has its own unique requirements and as such we offer tailored cleaning and hygiene solutions to meet the needs of each and every business environment. From general office for small and large businesses, SJADU Solutions is ready to support.

Hygiene Services

We understand that hygiene is not the only focus for an office environment and therefore we also provide site enhancement add-ons such as plant and planters, where we either offer once off purchases or monthly rental options.

Outdoor and Events Cleaning

Every event is about creating a welcoming atmosphere that is suitable for the desired clientele. Whether outdoor office environment or events.
